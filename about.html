About This Website

This portfolio was created to showcase my academic background, skills, and projects as a GIS and Remote Sensing student. It serves as a central place where lecturers, peers, and potential employers can learn more about my work in spatial analysis, land use classification, and geospatial problem-solving.

Through this site, you can explore my biodata, academic details, the tools and technologies I work with, and the innovative projects I have undertaken — including my Google Earth Engine LULC classification and ArcGIS Pro suitability analysis work in Kisumu County, Kenya.

Feel free to browse through the pages, and reach out via the Contact page if you'd like to connect or collaborate.
